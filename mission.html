HOW CAN I HELP?
Join us!

By cleaning oceans and seas from the plastic through our kits and divided into groups of volunteerings. OLA DEL MAR can be your new family to help our planet. Do you want to pass the eco message to your kid? come to our weekend cleaning rendez-vous, it’s open to all the family! Do you have a low budget to travel this summer? Check our beaches locations and you can select your summer home and go to food festivals for free!
